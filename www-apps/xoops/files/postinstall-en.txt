You will need to

 chmod 666 ${MY_INSTALLDIR}/mainfile.php

Then browse to http://${VHOST_HOSTNAME}${VHOST_APPDIR}

When you are done with the installer:

 rm -rf ${MY_INSTALLDIR}/install
 chmod 444 ${MY_INSTALLDIR}/mainfile.php
